The target audience is business owners in the Mesa area. These individuals own small businesses and their businesses are based in the Mesa area. These people are here to build connections within the community to both help their own business and build up others. The members of the community come here to better network within one group. Our goal is to bring these people together so that not only them, but the community that they help support is lifted up.

  / Kyle J. Norton / Arizona
